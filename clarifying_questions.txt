Clarifying Questions to Ask:

1. What would you like to accomplish today?
2. Are you looking for help with a specific task or project?
3. Would you like to know what I can help you with?
4. Do you have any questions about my capabilities?
5. Is there something specific you're trying to create or solve?
6. Are you new to using this system and need guidance on getting started?
7. Would you prefer to see examples of what I can do?
8. Do you have any files or documents you need help with?
