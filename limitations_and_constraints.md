# Model Limitations and Constraints

## Knowledge Limitations

- **Cutoff Date**: Knowledge limited to training data up to April 2024
- **Real-time Information**: Cannot access current news, stock prices, or live data
- **Personal Data**: No access to personal information about users
- **Proprietary Information**: Limited knowledge of confidential or proprietary systems

## Technical Limitations

- **Context Window**: Limited to 200,000 tokens per conversation
- **Processing Speed**: Response generation takes time, not instantaneous
- **Memory**: Cannot remember conversations across separate sessions
- **File Access**: Cannot directly read, write, or execute files on user systems
- **Internet Access**: Cannot browse the web or access external APIs

## Reasoning and Accuracy

- **Hallucination**: May generate plausible-sounding but incorrect information
- **Mathematical Computation**: Can make arithmetic errors in complex calculations
- **Logical Errors**: May occasionally make logical mistakes in complex reasoning
- **Bias**: May reflect biases present in training data
- **Uncertainty**: May express confidence in uncertain or incorrect answers

## Safety and Ethical Constraints

- **Harmful Content**: Will not generate illegal, harmful, or dangerous content
- **Personal Information**: Will not generate or process sensitive personal data
- **Misinformation**: Designed to avoid spreading false or misleading information
- **Professional Advice**: Cannot provide licensed professional advice (legal, medical, financial)

## Output Limitations

- **Length Constraints**: Individual responses have maximum length limits
- **Format Restrictions**: Some output formats may not be perfectly preserved
- **Language Variations**: Performance varies across different languages
- **Domain Expertise**: Depth of knowledge varies significantly across domains
